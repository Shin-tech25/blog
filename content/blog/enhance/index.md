## Meaning

_Enhance_ means **to improve the quality, value, or attractiveness of something**.
It is often used when something becomes better, stronger, or more impressive due to an addition or modification.

## Grammar and Usage

- **Part of speech:** Verb
- **Transitivity:** Transitive (requires an object)

**Structure:**
`enhance + noun`
e.g., _enhance performance_, _enhance beauty_, _enhance reputation_

**Common patterns:**

- _enhance the quality/value of something_
- _be designed to enhance something_
- _help to enhance something_

## Common Phrases

- enhance performance
- enhance efficiency
- enhance appearance
- enhance user experience
- enhance reputation

## Collocations

- **Verb + enhance:** help enhance, aim to enhance, try to enhance
- **Adjective + noun:** enhanced version, enhanced features
- **Noun + enhancement:** performance enhancement, security enhancement

## Examples

1. The new software update is designed to **enhance system performance**.
2. Good lighting can **enhance the appearance** of a room.
3. This training program will **enhance your communication skills**.
4. The company is working hard to **enhance its reputation** in the global market.
5. Adding a pinch of salt will **enhance the flavor** of the dish.
6. The new camera lens **enhances image clarity** significantly.
7. The app was updated to **enhance user experience**.
8. These changes are expected to **enhance overall efficiency**.
