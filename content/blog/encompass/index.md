## Meaning

**Encompass** means _to include a wide range of things_ or _to completely surround something_. It expresses the idea of covering or containing various elements within a whole.

## Grammar and Usage

- **Part of speech:** Verb
- **Type:** Transitive (requires an object)

**Patterns:**

- _encompass something_
- _be encompassed by something_

**Typical structures:**

- _encompass a wide range of topics_
- _be encompassed by mountains_

## Common Phrases

- encompass a broad spectrum
- encompass all aspects
- be encompassed by nature
