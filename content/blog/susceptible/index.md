## Meaning

_“Susceptible”_ means **easily influenced, affected, or harmed by something**.
It describes a person or thing that is **sensitive** or **vulnerable** to certain influences or conditions.

## Grammar and Usage

- **Part of speech:** Adjective
- **Common patterns:**

  - susceptible **to** something
  - be susceptible **to + noun/gerund**

**Examples of usage patterns:**

- susceptible to disease
- susceptible to flattery
- susceptible to damage
- susceptible to cold weather

## Common Phrases

- **susceptible to infection** – easily get sick
- **susceptible to influence** – easily influenced by others
- **susceptible to stress** – sensitive to stressful situations
- **highly susceptible to** – strongly affected by something

## Collocations

| Verb + Susceptible       | Noun + Susceptible     |
| ------------------------ | ---------------------- |
| become susceptible       | person susceptible     |
| make someone susceptible | population susceptible |
| remain susceptible       | area susceptible       |

## Examples

1. Children are more **susceptible to colds** than adults.
2. People who lack sleep are **susceptible to stress**.
3. The old wooden bridge is **susceptible to damage** during storms.
4. She’s **susceptible to flattery**, so people often praise her to get favors.
5. Some plants are **susceptible to frost** in early spring.
6. Weak immune systems make individuals **susceptible to infection**.
7. Emotional people are sometimes **susceptible to manipulation**.
8. The region is **susceptible to earthquakes**.

## Synonyms or Related

## Antonym

- resistant
- immune
- insusceptible
