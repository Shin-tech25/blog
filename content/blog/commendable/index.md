## Meaning

**Commendable** means **deserving praise or approval** because something is done well, thoughtfully, or with good intentions.
It expresses admiration for actions or qualities that are worthy of respect.

## Grammar and Usage

- **Part of speech:** Adjective
- **Used to describe:** behavior, effort, attitude, or performance that deserves praise.
- **Typical structure:**

  - It is _commendable_ that + clause
  - _Commendable_ + noun (e.g., commendable effort)

**Examples of structure:**

- _It is commendable that she volunteers every weekend._
- _His commendable performance earned him a promotion._

## Common Phrases

- **commendable effort** — an effort worth praising
- **commendable attitude** — a positive or responsible attitude
- **commendable behavior** — good conduct or actions
- **commendable initiative** — taking positive action on one’s own
